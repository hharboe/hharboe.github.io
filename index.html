Hi, I’m Henry, a “product person” in Brooklyn, NY. My background is in design consulting, with an emphasis on research and information architecture. I also founded an edtech startup.

   I left my consulting job one year ago to take on my first freelance product design contract. Now, I’m looking to join a product team, where my background will be valuable, and I can learn from experienced user researchers and designers.

   You can explore my work, browse my journal, or read more about me below. Thanks for visiting!

MISSION

I've found I'm happiest when helping people become their best selves. I think this starts with access to relevant education, and high-leverage tools. I like my work to focus on this, at scale, whenever possible.

MUSIC

Music has always been big for me. I studied jazz piano, saxophone, and guitar from grade school through college, and hosted a radio show on WOBC for four years. I still write and record new music; you can follow me on SoundCloud.

FOCUS

I always knew I loved the problems of product design, but it took years of course-correction as a founder, consultant, and now freelancer to understand where I best fit in.

   In a room of consultants, I’m advocating for end-users. In a room of engineers, I’m focusing on business objectives. Understanding why a product works, and where it should go next, is what really makes me tick.

   I've never worked within a large product team, and I'm still unsure where I fit between design, research, and product management. That said, I've never been more excited to devote time to a craft.

FUN

I’m not always this serious! I love hanging out in NYC parks and delis. I've never purchased an avocado toast. I'd probably love to meet ya!
