About Me


I'm an ex-startup founder, product manager, and designer in Brooklyn. I grew up in the city of Chicago, and studied Economics at Oberlin.

I'm driven to build tools that empower others to become their best selves. I've worked towards this by founding an edtech company, and working for a few social impact startups.

Academically, I'm interested in complex systems science, and the psychology of habit. I want to find ways to apply complexity science methods to more product problems!

When I'm not working, you'll probably find me recording music at home, or out exploring NYC.
