About Me

Mission

In life and in design, I'm happiest when helping others reach their full potential. I enjoy building tools that educate and empower.

Background

Since graduating from Oberlin College in 2015, I've been a behavioral economics consultant, product data analyst, and product manager, researcher, and designer.

Along the way, I founded an edtech startup, where I led design, marketing, and sales part-time from 2013-2015, and full-time in 2016.

Complex Systems

I attended my first NECSI workshop in January, which hooked me on the growing field of Complex Systems Science. A personal goal for 2018 is to better grasp the interdisciplinary methods of CSS, and apply them in my work.

Music

I wrote for an indie music magazine in high school, and hosted an experimental radio show on WOBC 91.5 FM for four years. I also record my own music, and post some of it on SoundCloud.
