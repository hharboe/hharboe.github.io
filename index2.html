Hi, I’m Henry, a “product person” in Brooklyn, NY. My background is in design consulting, with an emphasis on research, statistics, and information architecture. I also founded an edtech startup.


I left my consulting job one year ago to take on my first freelance product design contract. Now, I’m looking to join a product team, where my background will be valuable, and I can learn from more experienced researchers and designers.


You can explore my work, browse my journal, or read more about me below. Thanks for visiting!

MISSION

I find I'm happiest when helping people grow to become their best selves. In design, I believe this begins with personalized, discoverable education; and high-leverage, user-friendly tools.

MUSIC

Music has always been big in my life. I studied piano, saxophone, and guitar from grade school through college, and hosted a radio show on WOBC 91.5 FM for four years. I still write and record new music, and you can follow me on SoundCloud.

FOCUS

I've always loved working on design and research problems, but it took me years as a founder, consultant, and now freelancer to understand where I best fit in.


In a room of consultants, I’m advocating for end-users. In a room of engineers, I’m focusing on business objectives. I love using research and modeling to understand why a product works, and where it should go next.


I've never worked within a large product team, and I'm still unsure where I belong between design and user research. That said, I've never been more excited to devote my time to a craft.

FUN

I’m not always this serious! I love hanging out in NYC parks and bodegas. I've never purchased an avocado toast. I'd probably love to meet ya!
